# Welcome to My Blog

Hello! I'm Mark, and this is my personal blog where I'll be sharing my journey through computer science, competitive programming, and various tech adventures.

## What to Expect

I'll be writing about:

- **Competitive Programming**: Tips, tricks, and problem-solving strategies from my experience in Codeforces and USACO

- **Project Updates**: Cool things I'm building
- **Tech Thoughts**: General musings about technology and programming
